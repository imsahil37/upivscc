\section{Chapter Scheme}

The proposed dissertation will be organized into the following chapters:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Chapter 1: Introduction}
    \newline
    This chapter will introduce the research topic, providing a historical context of money and payment systems. It will outline the problem statement, research questions, objectives, and the significance of the study.

    \item \textbf{Chapter 2: Literature Review}
    \newline
    A comprehensive review of existing literature on digital payments, credit cards, and UPI. It will cover theoretical frameworks (Two-sided markets, TAM) and empirical studies on adoption drivers and barriers.

    \item \textbf{Chapter 3: Research Methodology}
    \newline
    Detailed explanation of the research design, sampling techniques, data collection instruments, and statistical tools used. It will also discuss the validity and reliability of the instruments.

    \item \textbf{Chapter 4: Data Analysis and Interpretation}
    \newline
    Presentation of secondary data analysis (RBI/NPCI trends) and primary data analysis (Survey results). This chapter will include hypothesis testing and interpretation of findings.

    \item \textbf{Chapter 5: Findings, Conclusion, and Recommendations}
    \newline
    Summary of key findings, conclusion of the study, and strategic recommendations for stakeholders (Banks, Fintechs, Policymakers). It will also outline the limitations and scope for future research.
\end{enumerate}
