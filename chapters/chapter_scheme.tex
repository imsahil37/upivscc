\section{Chapter Scheme}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Introduction:} Evolution of payments, Four-Party Model, UPI Revolution, Credit on UPI concept, and Problem Statement.
    \item \textbf{Review of Literature:} Theoretical frameworks (TAM, UTAUT), global parallels (Pix, Alipay), and Indian context.
    \item \textbf{Research Methodology:} Research design, sampling, data collection, and statistical tools.
    \item \textbf{Data Analysis (Macro-Economic):} Trend analysis of UPI vs. Card volumes, infrastructure asymmetry, and ATS analysis.
    \item \textbf{Data Analysis (Behavioral):} Consumer and merchant survey analysis, demographic profiling, and hypothesis testing.
    \item \textbf{Findings, Recommendations, and Conclusion:} Summary of findings, managerial/policy implications, and future research directions.
\end{enumerate}
