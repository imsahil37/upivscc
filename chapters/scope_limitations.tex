\section{Scope and Limitations}

\subsection*{Scope}
The study covers Pan-India secondary trends and Delhi-NCR primary data from FY 2020-21 to 2024-25. It focuses on retail POS transactions, excluding B2B and online CNP transactions.

\subsection*{Limitations}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Data Nascent Stage:} ``Credit on UPI'' is new; long-term data is limited.
    \item \textbf{Regional Bias:} Urban Delhi-NCR findings may not fully apply to rural India.
    \item \textbf{RuPay Exclusivity:} Current restriction to RuPay limits generalizability to Visa/Mastercard users.
    \item \textbf{Sample Size:} 250 respondents is a limitation for broader generalization.
    \item \textbf{Rapid Evolution:} The fast-paced fintech landscape may render some findings transient.
\end{itemize}
